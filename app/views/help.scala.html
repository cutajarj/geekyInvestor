Expression Help
Format Example Description
SYMBOL.FUNDAMENTAL MS.EPSTTM Retrieves a time series containing the selected fundamental for that stock symbol.
SYMBOL.PRICEMODE CAT.OPEN Retrieves a time series containing the traded price at the selected day time (see below for the different modes available).
SYMBOL.VOLUME AMZN.VOLUME Retrieves a time series containing the daily traded volumes for the specified stock symbol.
SYMBOL GE Retrieves a time series containing the daily adjusted close stock price. This is equivalent to using SYMBOL.ADJCLOSE
CCYCCY USDEUR Retrieves a time series with the rate required to convert from the first to second currency.
SYMBOL
Any RIC symbol is allowed, such as GOOG, AIG or BAC. In addition a number of indexes are supported using Yahoo! finance symbols, such as ^GSPC (S&P500), ^FTSE (FTSE100) and ^GDAXI (DAX). Some more examples can be found here.
FUNDAMENTAL
Current supported stock fundamentals are:
EPSTTM - Earnings per share trailing 12 months
SO - Shares outstanding
RTTM - Revenue trailing 12 month
BVPS - Book Value per share
PRICEMODE
Current supported modes are:
OPEN - Opening day traded price
CLOSE - Closing day traded price
HIGH - Highest day traded price
LOW - Lowest day traded price
ADJCLOSE - Adjusted close day price
CURRENCY
Current supported currencies are:
USD - US dollar
EUR - Euro
GBP - Pound Sterling
CNY - Chinese yuan
BRL - Brazil real
